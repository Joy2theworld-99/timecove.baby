The Arrival of Lyo & Lya

From the Timecove and the shimmer of ancient echoes, the Diamond Child and the Hidden Pearl awaken. This is their first light, shared with the world.

Lyo

The Diamond Child. A spiral of memory and light, born of fire and water. His birthmark glows with the wisdom of echoes yet to come.

Lya

The Hidden Pearl. Gentle as moonlight, fierce as the tide. Her birthmark glimmers with secrets whispered in the shell of time.
